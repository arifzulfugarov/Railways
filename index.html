Game Rules

  • Set your name and choose the difficulty to start the game.
  • Easy difficulty is a 5x5 grid, and Hard is a 7x7 grid.
  • Your goal is to navigate the railway and reach the end as fast as possible.
  • Each move affects the route, so choose wisely!
  • The leaderboard shows who completes the map the fastest.
